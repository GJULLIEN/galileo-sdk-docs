Time the duration of the pulse in microseconds.

Read a pulse on a pin(HIGH or LOW), and returns the duration of the pulse in microseconds. Returns 0 if the timeout is exceeded while waiting for a pulse.

Parameters
[in]iPinPin number to read the pulse
[in]iValuePulse to read: HIGH or LOW
[in][ulTimeout]Wait time, in microseconds, for pulse start; 1 second default
Returns
The duration of the pulse in microseconds
